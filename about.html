About EduFun Library

Empowering minds through knowledge since 1985

Our Story

EduFun Library has been serving the community for over 35 years, providing access to knowledge, fostering learning, and creating a space where curiosity thrives. What started as a small neighborhood library has grown into a comprehensive learning center with thousands of books, digital resources, and educational programs.

Our mission is to democratize access to information and create an inclusive environment where everyone can explore, learn, and grow. We believe that books have the power to transform lives, spark imagination, and build stronger communities.

Our Mission

To provide free and equal access to information, promote literacy, and serve as a community hub for lifelong learning and cultural enrichment.

Our Vision

To be the leading library system that inspires discovery, creativity, and connection within our community through innovative services and resources.

Our Values

Community

Our History

1985

Foundation

EduFun Library was established with a collection of 5,000 books in a small building downtown.

1995

Expansion

We moved to our current location and doubled our collection to 10,000 books.

2005

Digital Age

Introduced computer labs and internet access for public use.

2015

Modern Library

Launched our digital collection with e-books and online databases.

2024

Present Day

Now serving over 5,000 members with 15,000+ books and comprehensive digital resources.
