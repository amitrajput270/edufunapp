2. Library Membership

2.1 Eligibility

Library cards are available to:

  • Residents of our service area with valid proof of address
  • Students enrolled in local educational institutions
  • Employees of participating organizations
  • Temporary residents with valid identification

2.2 Registration Requirements

To register for a library card, you must:

  • Provide valid government-issued photo identification
  • Provide current proof of address
  • Complete the registration form accurately
  • Agree to these Terms and Conditions
  • Be at least 18 years old, or have parental consent if under 18

2.3 Card Responsibilities

Library cardholders are responsible for:

  • Maintaining current contact information
  • Safeguarding their library card and account information
  • All materials borrowed on their card
  • All activities conducted using their card or account
  • Reporting lost or stolen cards immediately

2.4 Card Renewal and Expiration

Library cards expire annually and must be renewed with current information. Expired cards may result in suspension of borrowing privileges.

3. Borrowing Policies

3.1 Loan Periods

  • Books: 3 weeks (renewable once if no holds)
  • DVDs: 1 week (not renewable)
  • Magazines: 1 week (not renewable)
  • Reference materials: In-library use only
  • Digital materials: Varies by format (1-3 weeks)

3.2 Loan Limits

  • Books: 10 items per card
  • DVDs: 3 items per card
  • Digital materials: 5 items per card
  • Total combined limit: 15 items per card

3.3 Renewals

Items may be renewed once if:

  • No other patron has placed a hold on the item
  • Your account is in good standing
  • The item is eligible for renewal
  • You renew before the due date

3.4 Holds and Reservations

Patrons may place holds on items that are currently checked out. Hold notifications are sent via email or phone. Reserved items must be picked up within 7 days of notification.

4. Overdue Items and Fines

4.1 Overdue Fines

  • Books: $0.25 per day per item
  • DVDs: $1.00 per day per item
  • Magazines: $0.50 per day per item
  • Maximum fine per item: Cost of replacement

4.2 Grace Period

A 7-day grace period applies to all materials before fines begin accruing.

4.3 Lost or Damaged Items

Patrons are responsible for the full replacement cost of lost or damaged items, plus a $5.00 processing fee. Damaged items may be repaired at the patron's expense if repair is possible.

4.4 Account Suspension

Borrowing privileges may be suspended if:

  • Fines exceed $25.00
  • Items are overdue by more than 30 days
  • Account information is outdated
  • Terms and Conditions are violated

5.1 Access Requirements

Digital services require:

  • Valid library card in good standing
  • Compatible device and internet connection
