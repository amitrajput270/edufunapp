Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a library card?

Visit us in person with a valid ID and proof of address. Library cards are free for all residents.

How long can I borrow books?

Books can be borrowed for 3 weeks and can be renewed once if no one else has requested them.

Do you have late fees?

We have a grace period of 7 days. After that, there's a small daily fee to help maintain our collection.

Can I access digital books?

Yes! Library members have free access to our digital collection of e-books and audiobooks.

Do you host events?

We regularly host book clubs, author readings, workshops, and children's story times. Check our events calendar!

Can I reserve study rooms?

Yes, we have several study rooms available for reservation. You can book them online or call us.
